About Us

Hope is a Christian organization that addresses the physical, emotional and spiritual needs of the people around the world

Our History

The world is sick. A lot of people around in this planet have an enormous amount of endurance, hope and dignity. They need help to fight against poverty, diseases,injustice even unemployment.

Hope was founded in 2002. Headquartered in Union New Jersey, we are a non-profit organization containing the following EIN Number:00-0000000. This organization has moved beyond the failed models of charity. We inspire hope and we create hope models that allow people to create their own hope for their futures We believe the only (greatest) help is to permit others to develop their own lives so that they are free of outside support.

Our Work

Hope provides clean water,transportation, agriculture, huge housing and excellent financing opportunities in many underdeveloped countries.
A lot of poor countries have gone though hurricanes, cholera, civil unrest and even earthquake. We have not stayed indifferent. We have empowered the suffered. Everyday because of our exceptional work, someone gets a better future in this world. Hope reaches out to every single person that needs help in this world. Hope is the solution.
